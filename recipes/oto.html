OTO


Oto a Ghanaian made dish but commonly eaten by Nigerians.

It is a sumptuous meal and quite easy to prepare provided you have the right Ingredients.

It is best prepared with palm oil instead of any other prefered oil. Also, in your choice of protein, chicken wont be bad but eggs would do perfectly.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of mashed yams
  • Two tablespoons grated onions
  • 1/2 cup of palm official
  • 1 ripe, peeled tomato
/li>
  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • Salt and pepper

    • Steps

    1. Boil the yams and mash smoothly using a folk
    2. Prepare a sauce in a saparete saucepan by frying onions in palm oil, add salt and pepper to taste.
    3. If you prefer, you can add the tomato then remove the pan from the heat source.
    4. Take 2 yolks from the boiled eggs, mash and stir into the sauce blend.
    5. Then mix the blend into the mashed yams until it has an even color.
    6. The dish is ready to be served. Place in a bowl use the rest of the boiled eggs to decorate.

    Bon appetite
